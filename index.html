A Letter I Never Quite Sent 💌

Dear Vaishnavi,

I want you to know — I never wanted to make you feel uncomfortable, not even for a single moment. That has never been, and will never be, my intention.

Your presence in my life made ordinary days feel genuinely lighter. I didn't plan for it. It just happened — quietly, naturally, the way all real things do.

I still value the bond we had. Every small conversation, every shared laugh, every quiet moment — none of it felt small to me. Even the tiniest gestures, things you probably don't remember, became little things I held onto.

I miss the old comfort between us — the kind where we could talk without overthinking. The kind where things felt easy.

I'm not asking for anything beyond that. Just honesty, and maybe someday — when you feel ready — a conversation.

With genuine warmth,
Someone who still cares.

Somewhere after March… things started feeling different.

I don't know what changed between us…
but I genuinely miss the way we used to talk.

"Can you give me a little of your time someday…
so we can talk properly once again?"

Made with genuine feelings, memories, and hope. 💗
